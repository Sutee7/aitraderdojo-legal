Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how AI Trader Dojo ("we", "our") handles information in connection with our website and our personal content automation tool (the "Service"). The Service is operated by a single individual and is used to publish the operator's own videos to the operator's own social media accounts.

1. Information We Collect

The Service collects only the minimum information required to function:

  • TikTok account information. When the operator authorizes the tool through TikTok Login Kit, TikTok provides basic account information (such as open ID, display name, and avatar) and OAuth access tokens with the user.info.basic and video.publish permissions.
  • Content metadata. Video titles, captions, and hashtags generated by the tool for the operator's own posts.

We do not collect personal information from website visitors. The website does not require registration and does not use tracking for advertising purposes.

2. How We Use Information

  • To authenticate with the TikTok API and publish the operator's own videos to the operator's own TikTok account.
  • To maintain the authorized session (token refresh) so uploads can run automatically.
The Service publishes content only to accounts owned by the operator of AI Trader Dojo. It does not access, collect, or process data belonging to any other TikTok user.

3. Data Storage and Security

OAuth tokens and content metadata are stored locally on the operator's own computer. They are not uploaded to any external server, database, or cloud service, and are not accessible to third parties.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share any information with third parties. Information is exchanged only with TikTok through its official API, as required to publish content, and such exchange is governed by TikTok's own Privacy Policy.

5. Data Retention and Revocation

Access tokens remain stored only while the automation tool is in use and can be deleted at any time. Authorization may also be revoked at any time from within the TikTok app under Settings → Security → Manage app permissions, which immediately invalidates the tool's access.

6. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect information from anyone under the age of 18.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page with an updated effective date.
